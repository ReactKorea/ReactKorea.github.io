About

React Seoul is a non-profit conference for React developers held by React Korea community.

React, which was first released in 2013 by Facebook, has been growing over years and was used in a lot of companies over the world including Korean ones. Although many developer conferences have React as a part of their program, none of them have covered the vast world of React to its full extent. So, we organized React Seoul ourselves to boost its ecosystem in Korea.

Whatever you want to know about the conference, fill free to ask on the #react-seoul channel in our slack.

This event is organized by community member volunteers.
